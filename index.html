ZigZag is a native, easy-to-use, reliable, fully secure and low fee Decentralized Exchange built on ZK Rollups.

Main Features

ZigZag's main features consist of the following three strengths.

ZK Rollups

ZigZag focuses purely on Zero-Knowledge technology by building on ZK Rollups. ZK Rollups are the end-game Layer 2 solution to the congested Ethereum mainnet.

These ZK Rollups will bundle transactions on the Layer 2 leading to quicker transaction speeds and negligible fees, whilst freeing up space on the mainnet.

Decentralized Orderbook

In a highly liquid market an orderbook is our choice of option compared to an AMM since scalability is a resolved issue with ZK Rollups. Furthermore, ZigZag's orderbook is decentralized allowing for users to trade in an instant, without a registration, whilst keeping control over their funds.

Bottomless Liquidity

With the support of top market makers in the crypto space, ZigZag provides bottomless liquidity. ZigZag guarantees that the liquidity is sufficient for any trader no matter their size, with minimal slippage and fees.
