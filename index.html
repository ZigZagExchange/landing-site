Institutional Scalability

Zigzag uses an off-chain order book that allows hundreds of limits orders per second to be processed without gas fees. Only matched trades hit the ZKSync chain.

Token

The ZAG token is Zigzag's native protocol token that aggregates all fees paid into the Zigzag protocol. Fees are free for maker orders and 3 bips for filled taker orders.

Token Distribution

The ZAG token will be distributed at a soon to be announced date to early users and market makers of the Zigzag protocol
